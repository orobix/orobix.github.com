We are Orobix.
We cross-breed artificial intelligence and engineering
to unlock new possibilities in the real world.

At Orobix we leverage the latest advances in artificial intelligence
and a strong engineering know-how to enable what was not possible before.

Deep learning, the technique that is revolutionizing the world, is at the heart of our solutions - we have a team dedicated to it.
On the other end, our engineers break down real-world problems and tailor our solutions to specific applications and end-users.
Our computer science team provides the technological foundation and makes sure our software is sound, scalable and resilient.

We engineer systems end-to-end, starting from a problem all the way to a solution working in production.
Our output is pure software, or a combination of software and hardware, such as sensors or robots.
