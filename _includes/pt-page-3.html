Modeling

We employ the tools of applied mathematics, engineering and computer science as means to understand reality. From biological processes to financial transactions, we adopt quantitative approaches to simulate phenomena or discover patterns, making a picture emerge from the canvas. We carefully evaluate modeling tradeoffs, balancing the sophistication of the mathematical description with the unavoidable uncertainties of realworld phenomena. We aim for as simple as possible, but not simpler.

Image Analysis

Images are ubiquitous in today's world, from medicine to manufacturing, from robotics to society. The ability to pick out quantitative information and semantic content from imaging data opens up endless possibilities for improving treatments, optimizing productive processes and enhancing lifestyles. Every image-based application has its specificities: we leverage on the latest developments in image processing and machine learning to provide focused solutions to specific problems.

Data Engineering

Data is everywhere. Events, finance, society, sensors have become sources of continuous streams of data that need to be managed, integrated and distilled into useful information for managing processes and making informed decisions. Our ability to design scalable infrastructures that incorporate ad-hoc data analysis algorithms empowers our customers with new means and makes data an opportunity, rather than a problem.
