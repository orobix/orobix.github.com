Application Design

Our ultimate goal is placing enabling technology in the hands of end-users, allowing them to manage complexity and expand their possibilities. We design our applications across all forms, web, desktop and mobile, striving to maximize effectiveness and eliminate friction. Our experience in 3D and visualization allows our users to extract meaning out of raw data.

Infrastructure Design

We are passionate about creating the right infrastructure at the right scale, from single-instance applications to highly distributed systems on the cloud. We leverage the whole technological landscape around web-scale services, communication and database technologies, bridging high-performance computing, distributed data collection and analysis.

Software Development

We enjoy writing software and we keep our environment as dynamic and polyglot as possible. We have a predilection for functional programming, but we find ourselves equally at ease working on object-oriented code-bases. We design our software meticulously, making sure it's testable and resilient to failure right from the start. Our software is made to evolve with the world around it.
