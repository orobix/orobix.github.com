We are Orobix

We at Orobix are committed to finding practical solutions to real-world problems. From modeling reality using mathematical principles to building scalable infrastructures with state-of-the-art software engineering practices, we cover the whole process back to front. We partner with our customers to define the boundaries of a problem, we explore innovative methodologies to solve it and we craft the tools that enable end-users to apply those methodologies in practice. We do all this in a traceable way, embracing quality assurance practices as required by the diverse fields in which we operate.

Engineering usable tools

Our methodological approach allows us to operate in disparate contexts, from automated image analysis for pharmaceutical applications to numerical simulations of medical devices, from machine learning applied to industrial manufacturing to distributed architectures for real-time trading. Whatever the application, we commit to own the problem we're working on, bringing fresh views and added value to the table, reaching measurable results in timely and cost-effective steps. At Orobix we value the process that leads to a solution as much as the solution itself.
